# Outdoor World
## Objective:
- Mess around with lighting in the world by clicking the buttons
- Move around to explore the world
## Controls:
- ESC: unlock your cursor
- WASD: to move forwards, left, back, right
- Q: pan left
- E: pan right
- SPACE: fly up
- SHIFT: fly down
## Buttons: 
- Normal: shows the normals of the objects
- Orbit: the sun object orbits around the world
- Light: turns on and off the lighting in the room
- Spot: turns on and off the spotlight
- Night Mode: turns the sun into a moon
- Red Sun: turns the sun red
## Sliders:
- Light X: moves the sun in the X direction
- Light Y: moves the sun in the Y direction
- Light Z: moves the sun in the Z direction
- Sun R: increases or decrease the red value of the sun's lighting
- Sun G: increases or decrease the green value of the sun's lighting
- Sun B: increases or decrease the blue value of the sun's lighting
